TapGo Support

TapGo is a simple app that helps users quickly open Apple Maps transit directions to saved places or a destination shared by a helper.

Contact Support

For help, bug reports, or feedback, contact: tapgo_support@icloud.com

What to Include in a Support Email

To help resolve an issue faster, please include:

  • your iPhone model
  • your iOS version
  • what happened
  • steps to reproduce the issue
  • screenshots, if available

Frequently Asked Questions

How does TapGo work?

TapGo lets you save important places and open directions with fewer steps. It may also let a helper send a destination link that appears in the app for quick access.

Does TapGo require an account?

No. TapGo does not require account creation.

Does TapGo collect my data?

TapGo is designed to keep core app information local to your device. Please see the Privacy Policy for details.

Which map app does TapGo use?

TapGo is designed to work with Apple Maps for directions.

I received a destination from a helper. What do I do?

Open the TapGo link on your iPhone. The destination should appear in the app so you can launch directions more easily.

How do I remove a saved place?

Open the app and go to the place management area to rename or delete saved places.

Compatibility

TapGo is intended for supported iPhone devices running a compatible iOS version.
