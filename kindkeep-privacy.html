Privacy Policy — KindKeep

Last updated: April 2026

KindKeep is designed to be simple and private. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data.

KindKeep does not transmit any information to external servers, does not include analytics, and does not require an account.

Calendar access

KindKeep requests read and write access to your Apple Calendar in order to:

  • Save appointments you create to your default calendar.
  • Display your upcoming events for the next 3 days.

Calendar data is accessed on your device through Apple's EventKit framework. KindKeep does not transmit your calendar data anywhere. If you use iCloud Calendar, your events may sync across your Apple devices — that sync is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Natural language parsing

When you type an appointment description, KindKeep parses it entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks (NSDataDetector) and local pattern matching. No text you enter is sent to any server or third-party service.

Children's privacy

KindKeep does not knowingly collect information from children. The app collects no personal data from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact
