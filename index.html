TapGo

TapGo is a simple iPhone app that helps riders quickly open Apple Maps with transit directions to saved places or a destination shared by a helper.

About the App

TapGo is designed to reduce friction when starting a transit trip. Users can save important places and launch directions with fewer steps.

The app may also support receiving a destination through a TapGo link, so a helper can send a place that appears inside the app for easy access.
