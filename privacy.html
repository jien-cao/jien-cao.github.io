Privacy Policy for TapGo

Effective date: March 29, 2026

TapGo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information TapGo Collects

TapGo does not require users to create an account. TapGo does not provide a backend account system or cloud-hosted user database.

Information such as saved places and temporary shared destinations is intended to be stored locally on the device.

Location and Mapping

TapGo helps users open directions in Apple Maps and may use Apple mapping and search services to help users find destinations. When using Apple services, information may be handled by Apple according to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

Data Sharing

TapGo does not sell personal information. TapGo does not use third-party advertising networks. TapGo does not use third-party analytics or tracking for advertising purposes.

Links Shared Through the App

TapGo may allow a helper to create and share a destination link. When such a link is opened, destination information may appear in the app on the recipient’s device for temporary use.

Data Retention

Saved places remain on the user’s device until the user edits or deletes them. Temporary incoming destinations may be removed automatically by the app after a limited period or after use.

Your Choices

You can delete saved places from within the app. You can also remove the app from your device at any time.

Children’s Privacy

TapGo is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: tapgo_support@icloud.com
