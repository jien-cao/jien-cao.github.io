Privacy Policy — LittlePlotter

Last updated: April 2026

LittlePlotter is designed to be simple and private. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data.

LittlePlotter does not transmit any information to external servers, does not include analytics, and does not require an account.

Data stored on your device

LittlePlotter stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your data points — the X and Y values you enter, along with your current chart selection, are saved in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) so you can pick up where you left off. This data never leaves your device.

Photo library access

When you export a chart, LittlePlotter requests write-only permission to save an image to your photo library. The app cannot read your existing photos. This permission is requested only when you initiate an export.

In-app purchases

LittlePlotter offers optional tip purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. LittlePlotter does not receive or store any payment information.

Children's privacy

LittlePlotter does not knowingly collect information from children. The app collects no personal data from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: littleplot_support@icloud.com
